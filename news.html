A Heartwarming Day: Tadawena Team Brings Smiles to Orphans in Ard El-Golf Celebration

July 12, 2025

Today felt like a celebration for us as we met with the orphaned children.

As part of our social responsibility delegation, the Tadawena for Nutritional Supplements took part in the #Library_Cultural_Center_Ard_El-Golf event celebrating Orphan's Day, aiming to bring joy and happiness to the hearts of the children.
